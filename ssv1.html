SoundFont Shuffle Vol. 1

By Larry RMX

Original songs produced by various artists at Nintendo and Sega

SoundFont Shuffle Vol. 1 is the first in the SoundFont Shuffle series produced by Larry RMX. These remixes are produced using different soundfonts from various Nintendo games using many iconic Nintendo songs (and some from Sega too!).

Each remix from June 2025 featured on the Larry RMX channel is featured here, available in HD quality.

Please note these remixes are fan works made with love and are not intended to cause harm to Nintendo/Sega brands or profits in any way, shape, or form. Larry RMX does not claim ownership of any assets (including images and music) used in the creation of this album. All rights belong to Nintendo and Sega depening on the song. Larry RMX does not intended to harm either company and can remove this album if need be.
